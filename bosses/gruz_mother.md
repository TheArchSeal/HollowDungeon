*A large fly.*

*Large Beast*  
**AC** 13  
**HP** 37 (5d12 + 5)  
**Speed** 20 ft. Fly 30 ft.  
**Initiative** +3 (13)  

**Str** 12 (+1, +1)  
**Dex** 17 (+3, +3)  
**Con** 13 (+1, +1)  
**Int** 5  (-3, -3)  
**Wis** 5  (-3, -3)

**Mother.** On death: Summons 6 (1d4 + 4) [Gruzzers](/enemies/gruzzer.md).*
